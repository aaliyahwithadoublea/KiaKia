History

The history of printing is a journey of technological innovation and cultural transformation. It began with ancient techniques like woodblocks and stamps, but it was revolutionized by Johannes Gutenberg’s invention of the printing press with movable type in the 15th century.

Printing evolved over time, with advancements such as lithography, offset printing, and digital printing, each contributing to the accessibility and quality of printed materials. Overall, the history of printing showcases humanity’s ability to innovate and adapt, ultimately shaping the way we communicate, learn, and create in the modern world.

At KIAKIA PRINTS, our journey is woven with a rich history of passion, creativity, and dedication to the art of printing. Our story began in 2023, in the vibrant heart of Nigeria. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name in the printing industry.
